Hello there, my name is Jonathan and I see you've stumbled onto my website so here's a little bit about me!

Art has always been a escape for me, It's a way for me to express how I feel and I'm able to create any crazy idea that pops up in my mind. Along with art I'm a huge movie/ video game nerd, and I also collect Funko Pops. I'm also currently going to The Cleveland Institute of Art for Game Design!
